MENUS & SPECIALS

At the Country Gourmet of Mountain View, food is made fresh daily in our kitchen
using only the freshest & finest ingredients available. In case you didn't know, our food is
made to order, so if it takes us just a little bit longer in the kitchen - we thank you in advance
for your patience. We prefer fresh over frozen!

Our regular menu consists of items our customers regularly ask for and have
assurance when they come in, that their favorite items are still on the menu.
If it works for our customers, it works for us!

Catering Menu


The Country Gourmet of Mountain View is a modified self-service restaurant,
here are our ordering tips:

ORDERING TIPS

  • • Please place your order at the front counter before taking a seat.
  • • In addition to the menu items, specials are written on the blackboards, behind the registers.
  • • If you would like water, please help yourself near the soda station.
  • • If you’d like coffee and/or soda, once you order, cup/s will be provided and these beverages are then self-service. For drink refills please feel free to help yourself.
  • • If you ordered soup or salad and would like it before your meal please inform the cashier.
  • • Last but not least, please know it is our pleasure to serve you.


Enjoy your meal!
